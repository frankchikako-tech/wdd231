Site Plan: BaytaHome

Direct Rental Platform Connecting Landlords and Tenants in Nigeria

1. Site Name

BaytaHome

The name BaytaHome was chosen because it represents finding a “better home” experience in Nigeria. It is modern, brandable, and reflects trust in housing and rental services.

2. Site Purpose

The purpose of BaytaHome is to create a platform where tenants can discover verified rental homes and connect directly with landlords, reducing reliance on middlemen and extra agent fees.

The website will allow users to search for properties, filter by location and budget, view detailed listings, and send rental requests or inquiries directly to landlords.

3. Scenarios

  • How can I quickly find verified and affordable apartments in Abuja or Lagos?
  • How do I contact a landlord directly to confirm rent and schedule a viewing?

4. Color Schema

  • Primary Blue (#0b3d91): Used for headers, navigation bar, and footer.
  • Accent Orange (#f59e0b): Used for buttons and calls-to-action.
  • Success Green (#10b981): Used for confirmations and success messages.

5. Typography

The heading font is Poppins for a modern and professional look. The body font is Arial for simplicity and readability.

Example heading style uses Poppins (clean and bold branding style).

Example body text uses Arial for clear reading experience across devices.
